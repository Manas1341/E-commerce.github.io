Community Participation Guidelines

Our community is built on a foundation of mutual respect, openness, and inclusivity. We value the diversity of our community and are committed to creating an environment that is welcoming and safe for all.

Expected Behavior

  • Be respectful of others and their opinions.
  • Engage in constructive and respectful dialogue.
  • Use inclusive language and avoid personal attacks or harassment.
  • Listen actively and be willing to consider new ideas and perspectives.
  • Assume good faith and avoid making assumptions about others.

Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are considered unacceptable and will not be tolerated:

  • Harassment or bullying of any kind.
  • Hate speech or discrimination based on race, ethnicity, gender, sexual orientation, or any other personal characteristic.
  • Intimidation or threats.
  • Sharing of confidential or sensitive information without permission.
  • Any illegal activity or behavior.

Enforcement

Anyone who violates our community participation guidelines may be subject to disciplinary action, up to and including suspension or permanent ban from our community. We take all reports of unacceptable behavior seriously and will investigate them promptly.
